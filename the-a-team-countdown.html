Blanton's Single Barrel

Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey · 93 Proof · Buffalo Trace Distillery

The world's first commercially marketed single barrel bourbon, launched in 1984 by master distiller Elmer T. Lee. Made from Buffalo Trace's high-rye mash bill and aged in the legendary metal-clad Warehouse H, every pour delivers sweet caramel and vanilla up front, citrus and honey in the mid-palate, and a warm finish of toasted oak and baking spices. The iconic grenade-shaped bottle with its collectible horse-and-jockey stopper makes it as fun to display as it is to drink.

Bardstown Discovery Series #11

Blend of Straight Bourbon Whiskeys · 118.1 Proof · Bardstown Bourbon Co.

📍 Completed Thursday 12th March · The Old Yard, Kilternan

A masterful three-part blend: 73% thirteen-year-old Kentucky bourbon providing the backbone, 21% ten-year-old Kentucky bourbon for roundness, and 6% of Bardstown's own six-year wheated bourbon aged entirely in French oak. The result is a rich, layered pour — butterscotch and cherry on the nose, toffee and Christmas spices on the palate, finishing long with cinnamon, oak, and ripe red fruit. One of the Discovery Series' finest batches, and The A Team's inaugural bottle.
